## 2. Play Store Description

**Title:** project_phoenix — Charter & Human Rights Analyzer

**Short Description:**
Authoritative Canadian Charter and Human Rights analysis for legal research and statistics.

**Full Description:**
project_phoenix is a specialized research and statistics tool designed for Canadian legal professionals, students, and researchers. It provides a high-efficiency environment for analyzing documents for potential breaches of the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms and violations of the Canadian Human Rights Act (CHRA).

**Distribution and Access Notice (Publisher-Provided):**
* Open source software available for free download for all Canadians without a law license.
* This project is intended to remain available for free download permanently.
* Contributions to the developers and their descendants are always welcome via Interac e-Transfer to: **kalilife666@gmail.com**.
* Users with a law license require a paid subscription of **CAD $300/month**.

**Key Features:**
*   ⚖️ **Charter Breach Analysis:** Automated detection for Sections 2 through 15, including search and seizure (s.8), arbitrary detention (s.9), and right to counsel (s.10b).
*   🛡️ **Human Rights Detection:** Integrated analysis for CHRA violations, focusing on employment discrimination and prohibited grounds.
*   🚫 **Zero Tolerance Logic:** High-sensitivity detection for critical breach indicators, ensuring potential rights violations are never overlooked.
*   📚 **Legal Dictionary & Cross-Referencing:** Built-in authoritative definitions and direct integration with CanLII and the Criminal Law Notebook.
*   🤖 **Dual-AI Verification:** Optional GPT-4 or Google Gemini (1.5 Pro/Flash) integration to verify analysis accuracy and detect linguistic deflection or ambiguity.
*   📊 **Readability Statistics:** Automatic calculation of Flesch-Kincaid, Gunning Fog, and other readability indices to assess document complexity.
*   📄 **Professional Reporting:** Generate detailed HTML or Text reports with confidence scoring and legal test drill-downs.

**Research Focus:**
project_phoenix uses advanced keyword density mapping and "Zero Tolerance" confidence floors to provide statistical insights into legal document compliance. It is built to streamline the preliminary review process, allowing researchers to focus on the most critical sections of their documents.

## 3. Privacy Policy (Template)

**project_phoenix Privacy Policy**
*Last Updated: May 2024*

**1. Overview**
project_phoenix is committed to protecting the privacy of the legal documents you analyze. Our primary philosophy is local-first processing.

**2. Data Processing**
*   **Local Processing:** By default, all document analysis (keyword matching, dictionary cross-referencing, and Charter logic) is performed locally on your device. Your documents are not uploaded to our servers for core analysis.
*   **Third-Party APIs (Optional):** 
    *   **CanLII:** If you use the CanLII search feature, your search queries (usually Charter section numbers) are sent to CanLII's API.
    *   **OpenAI & Google Gemini:** If you enable AI Verification, text from your document is sent to the respective provider (OpenAI or Google). This is only done with your explicit consent and requires your own API key.
*   **No Data Retention:** We do not store, collect, or share the text of the documents you process through the app.

**3. Permissions**
*   **Storage/Files:** Required to load PDF, DOCX, and TXT documents from your device for analysis.
*   **Internet:** Required to access CanLII references and OpenAI verification features.

**4. Security**
We recommend using project_phoenix on encrypted devices and ensuring that any AI API keys used are kept confidential.
